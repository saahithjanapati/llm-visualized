Prism Multiplication Animation
- Two sets of instanced prisms will be visualized.
- The top vector's prisms will move down to align with the bottom vector's prisms.
- A flash effect will occur on the bottom vector.
- The bottom vector's prism colors will update based on the element-wise product of the two original vectors' data.
- The top vector's prisms will be "hidden" (moved off-screen) after the multiplication.
